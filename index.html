EK ONKAR SATGUR PARSAD
IN MEMORIUM

With the Blessing of Akal Purakh, This beautiful GURUDWARA was conceived in Memory of four Departed souls of Arora Family (Nagpur). Sardarni Babli Kaur, Sardarni Ruchika kaur, Sardarni Ritika Kaur and Sardar Raunak Singh who met with an accident on the Nagpur-Jabalpur NH7 at Bandol on 11th June 1991. The Family accepted this Tragedy as the will of the Akal Purakh and the Family head S.Gurcharan Singh Arora, Nagpur, met DR.Satnam Singh Nagpur (President, GURUMAT MISSION Jabalpur), conceived the idea to build this place where weary travelers could rest and satiate their soul with Guru's Langar and Recitation of Gurbani.

This place, Resting on 4 acres of land was built in association with GURUMAT MISSION and donated by the Arora Family, with Staguru's Grace and the combined efforts. GURUMAT MISSION and the Guru's Sangat is steadily growing into a GURUMAT Seminary and a Humanity service centre, providing peace and tranquility to all.
